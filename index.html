Ceylani Expo (Pvt) Ltd is a professionally managed export company headquartered in Trincomalee, dedicated to taking Sri Lanka's finest coastal and agricultural products to the world with integrity, consistency, and compliance.

Certifications & Compliance

We align with Sri Lankan export regulations and international food safety practices. Our partner facilities and supply chain follow recognized standards to support your compliance requirements.

ISO / HACCP Oriented

Partner facilities operate under ISO 22000 & HACCP guidelines for food safety.

EDB Registered

Compliant with Sri Lanka Export Development Board and relevant government authorities.

Lab Testing Support

Moisture, microbiology & residue tests via accredited laboratories on request.

Market-Specific Docs

Health, Halal & other certificates and supporting documents arranged for your target market.

Quality Assurance Process

Raw Material Inspection

Verification of source and initial quality assessment at collection points.

Processing Standards

Adherence to hygiene, handling, and processing protocols at partner facilities.

Laboratory Testing

Comprehensive testing for key quality parameters as per buyer requirements.

Final Inspection

Pre-shipment verification, packing inspection, and documentation review.

Our quality management approach ensures consistent product quality and compliance with international standards across all export markets we serve.
